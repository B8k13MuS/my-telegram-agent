# Vacation and Time-Off Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for paid time off (PTO).

## PTO Allowance

- **Annual leave**: 20 working days per year
- **Sick leave**: 10 working days per year
- **Public holidays**: As per national calendar
- **Additional days**: +1 day per year of service (max 5 extra days)

## Request Process

1. Submit request at least 2 weeks in advance via HR portal
2. Manager approval required (respond within 3 business days)
3. For sick leave: notify manager before 9:00 AM on first day
4. Track remaining balance in the HR portal

## Restrictions

- Maximum 15 consecutive days without director approval
- Blackout periods may apply (announced quarterly)
- Unused days: maximum 5 can be carried to next year
- PTO cannot be taken during the first month of employment

## Emergency Leave

For family emergencies or force majeure:
- Contact manager immediately
- Up to 3 days can be granted verbally
- Formal request must follow within 48 hours
