# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Eligible Expenses

The following expenses can be reimbursed with proper documentation:

- Business travel (flights, trains, taxis)
- Accommodation during business trips
- Meals during business travel (up to $50/day)
- Office supplies (under $100)
- Professional development (courses, conferences)
- Software subscriptions (with manager approval)

## Non-Reimbursable

- Personal expenses
- Alcohol (except team events with pre-approval)
- Traffic fines
- Personal vehicle maintenance
- Gifts for clients (require pre-approval)

## Submission Process

1. Collect all receipts (original or digital)
2. Fill expense report form in the finance portal
3. Attach receipts as PDF or images
4. Submit within 30 days of expense date
5. Manager approves within 5 business days
6. Finance processes reimbursement within 14 days

## Limits

| Category | Limit | Approval Required |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Office supplies | $100 | Manager |
| Software | $500/year | Director |
| Training | $2,000/year | Director |
| Travel | Actual cost | Manager |
