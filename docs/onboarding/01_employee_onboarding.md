# Employee Onboarding Process

## Overview

This document describes the standard employee onboarding process for new hires.

## Pre-Arrival (1 Week Before)

1. HR prepares the workspace and equipment
2. IT creates accounts:
   - Corporate email (firstname.lastname@company.com)
   - Slack account
   - GitLab/GitHub access
   - VPN credentials
3. Manager assigns a buddy/mentor

## Day 1

1. Welcome meeting with HR (9:00 AM)
2. Office tour and introductions
3. Equipment setup with IT support
4. Sign required documents:
   - NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)
   - Employment contract
   - IT security policy acknowledgment
5. Lunch with the team

## First Week

- Complete mandatory training modules (compliance, security)
- Set up development environment
- Review team documentation and coding standards
- Attend team standup meetings
- First 1:1 with direct manager

## First Month

- Complete first assigned task (small, well-defined)
- Receive feedback on initial work
- Attend department all-hands meeting
- Complete probation period goals document

## Contact

For questions about onboarding, contact:
- HR: hr@company.com
- IT Support: it-support@company.com
